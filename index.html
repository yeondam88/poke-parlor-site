Our Story

Your Dining Restaurant since 1989

Poké Parlor is the leading poke restuarant in La Habra.

History

We come from a background in sushi, which means we have an intimate understanding of how to bring out the best flavors in raw fish. We've applied this expertise to Poke Parlor, which serves extremely fresh ingredients and creative sauces to give each poke bowl and burrito its mouth-watering flavors. Stop by some time -- we can't wait for you to experience our delicious, healthy food for yourself!

Meet the Manager

Sun loves what he does, and his passion for serving memorable food defines his personality. He treats each guest like he would a close friend, making sure they get exactly what they want with his friendly demeanor. He also loves getting to know the people who visit us, so if you see him while you're here, be sure to say hello.
